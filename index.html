Salmon Cookies

Salmon Cookies celebrates the culture of the Pacific Northwest by combining two of our favorite things: Coffie and Salmon!

Our cookies are made using a special process that infuses them with the awesome flavor of freshly caught Northwest salmon. Varieties include:

  • Crispy Chinook
  • Savory Sockeye
  • Yummy Chum
  • And more!
